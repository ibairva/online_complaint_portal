Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is the purpose of Online Complaint Portal?

Online Complaint Portal is a site to help people report, view, or discuss local problems they’ve found to their local municipal corporation without standing up in a long queue.

2. What sort of problems should I report with this Online Complaint Portal?

This Online Complaint Portal is primarily for reporting things which are broken or dirty or damaged or dumped, and need fixing, cleaning or clearing, such as:
  • Abandoned vehicles
  • Litter
  • Streetcleaning or Pothole related problems
  • Fixing StreetLights

3. What isn’t this Online Complaint Portal for?

This online Complaint Portal is not a way of getting in touch with the Municipal Corporation for all issues. People will need to contact your Municipal Co-orperation directly for problems such as:
  • Anti-social behaviour
  • Any urgent or emergency problems
  • Noise pollution or barking dogs
  • Fires and smoke/smell pollution
  • Proposals for speed bumps/ CCTV/ pedestrian crossings/ new road layouts/ etc.
  • Complaining about neighbours
  • Complaining about the Municipal Coorperation

4. How do I use the site?

  • All complains shows all the complains reported and provides an extra functionality of sorting and filtering by places as well as type of complain.
  • To complain:
    • It is necessary to Login to Report any Complain.
    • After Login Description, Title , Type of Complains and all necessary details.
  • LeaderBoard shows the upvotes given to the reported complains.

5. How are the problems solved?

The reported complains are then passed on to the Middlemen who accepts/rejects the complains after validating it. The accepted complains are passed on the Municipal Coorporation and then the government provides a time track for the complain to be solved.You get an email notification by the Middlemen if your complain is accepted/rejected.
